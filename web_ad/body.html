About Auto Response

AutoResponse lets you customize your phone to be aware of it's environment.
You define sets of conditions and how you would like your phone to respond
if these conditions are met.

For example, the conditions might be that is is between 8am and 5pm on a
weekday and your location matches your office location. In this case, you
might configure the response to put your phone on silent mode so you are
not inturrupted.
